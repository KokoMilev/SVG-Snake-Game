Snake Game

How to Play

Control the snake to eat food and grow longer. Avoid hitting the walls or your own tail!

Objective

  • Eat food to grow and increase your score
  • Try to achieve the highest score possible
  • Don't crash into walls or yourself

Game Mechanics

  • The snake moves continuously in the current direction
  • Use arrow keys or WASD to change direction
  • Press Space to pause/resume or restart after game over
  • Press ESC to open this menu

Gameplay Tips

ğŸ¯ Strategy Tips

  • Plan Your Route: Think ahead to avoid getting trapped
  • Use the Grid: The grid lines help you plan precise movements
  • Stay in the Center: Avoid edges when possible to give yourself more options
  • Watch Your Tail: Your tail grows with each food eaten, so plan accordingly

ğŸ Food Strategy

  • Cherry (100 pts): Basic food - safe to eat anytime
  • Banana (120 pts): Slows you down - eat when you need more control
  • Coconut (150 pts): Provides shield - great for risky situations
  • Pineapple (200 pts): Double points - maximize score when safe
  • Pizza (400 pts): Increases speed - eat when you're confident
  • Mushroom (350 pts): Inverts controls - be very careful!
